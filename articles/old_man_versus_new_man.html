Our Mission

We are a not-for-profit organisation based in the United Kingdom that simply wants to raise the world-wide banner that "Jesus Christ is Lord."

We believe that God's Holy Bible is not just something you read, it's very much something you live, breath and do by God's grace for His glory.

We want Jesus to be at the centre of everything we do.

We seek to glorify God and to help others to do so by knowing Jesus, imitating Jesus, serving Jesus and sharing Jesus to make Him known.

Our Intentions

We hope that the content presented on this website might be an inspiration to all those seeking to follow Jesus for God's glory.

We want to help you make the bible part of your everyday living - both in word and deed.

The ToDo list items and topics are not presented as a legalistic list of Christian "do's and don'ts." Neither are they intended to promote justification by works.

The ToDo list seeks to offer readers a chance to learn and be challenged by the scriptures in practical ways so that they might increase in the knowledge of God and go on to demonstrate good works and obedience as a result of their faith.

Often the ToDo items and topics point to spiritual disciplines that may help the reader to deepen and strengthen their relationship with God. Others serve as faithful warnings, friendly encouragements and reminders of what the bible tells us to do.

Why not make your bible your ToDo list? Read it. Believe it. Do it! All for God's glory.

Our Reliance On Jesus

We acknowledge first and foremost that without Jesus we can do nothing. We rely on Him and His grace for all that we do.

Jesus said:

“without me ye can do nothing” John 15:5

We must say to all readers from the outset: don't "try" to keep Jesus' commandments and instructions in your own intrinsic "power", for you are powerless to carry them out.

These are "impossible" standards for fleshly men, but are imminently possible for those who learn to surrender to and engage their indwelling omnipotent helper the Holy Spirit.

The Christian who successfully lives by God's word is one who is continually filled with and controlled by the Spirit (Ephesians 5:18) and who continually walks by the Spirit (Galatians 5:16).

The supernatural lifestyle can only be lived out by relinquishing self effort and by relying continually on our supernatural source, the Holy Spirit's dunamis (His inherent power) to accomplish Jesus' commands and instructions.

Beware of the natural tendency of our fallen flesh to deceive us into thinking we can do any of the commands and instructions in our own energy, lest we fall into the subtle trap of legalism!
