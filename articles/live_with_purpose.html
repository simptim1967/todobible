Live With Purpose

Be encouraged. God has a purpose for your life.

You Were No Accident Of Birth

You were not an accident of birth or born by chance. You were fearfully and wonderfully made in secret by God. Your members were designed and documented by Him before ever you were formed in the womb.

You were born for this precise time!

You Are Not A Loser

You are not a loser. Nor does your past make you a failure. Stop beating yourself up.

God made you in His own image. He loves you unconditionally for exactly who you are regardless of anything you have done. God does not make mistakes!

God Thinks About You

God has never stopped thinking about you. His precious thoughts for you are inumerable. If you were to count them they would be greater in number than the grains of sand on a beach.

God Has A Plan For You

God has always had a plan for your life from the very beginning (see Jeremiah 29:11).

Jesus Can Wash You Clean And Set You Free

Jesus died to wash you clean. When you are washed with the blood of the lamb, all guilt and shame is washed away and forgotten. God wants you to focus on your future, not your past. He is prepared to completely forgive and forget your past sins.

One Life

We each have one life. This life counts. It is not a dress rehearsal.

Will you spend your life watching other peoples' lives and forget to live your own?

God created you to have your own life, your own adventure.

Stop spectating and start living. Don't settle for the mundane or mediocre. Aim for extraordinary!

God Wants Relationship With You

The Christian life is a relationship with God. Relationships work by spending time together in conversation. Conversations work when two people are both listening and speaking.

For our relationship to work well with God we have to have continually hearing ears to hear what He is saying. It's not good enough to know what He has said but rather we must hear what He is saying right now.

God is looking for individuals. He is looking down on the children of men to see if there are any who have understanding, to see if there are any who seek God (see Psalm 53:2).

God desires and always intended this thing we call "Christianity" to be a living, dynamic relationship with you every single day. Not out of duty but out of delight and love for Him.

There is never a day when God doesn't want to speak to you. There is not a moment when He is not speaking or a moment when He is too busy to speak with you because He is dealing with somebody else!

Today He has new things He wants to say to you. Today He wants to reveal new measures of His love to you. He wants to impact your emotions, impact your heart. He wants to come close to you.

But we are creatures of habit and we easily turn off. We are easily distracted and slip back into our routines. The suddenly we awaken and we say "God are you still there? Are you saying anything to me?" And then we feel the warmth of His presence come again and we settle down again. But He wants us to stay close all the time.

God Wants To Spend Time With You

Your quiet time with God is not just about your own spiritual growth. People who love each other willingly spend time together. It gives God pleasure to spend time with you. He redeemed you at great cost and wants to have fellowship with you. Don't deny Him that after all that He has done for you.

Bring Purpose To Your Life

Establishing a relationship with God through prayer and by reading the bible will help you to discover exactly what your unique purpose is and to live out your divine mandate abundantly to God's glory.

Don't be discouraged if it takes a while to discover your purpose. Just keep yourself busy working and wait patiently and faithfully upon God to reveal it to you.

God Wants To Speak Specifically To You

God has always been a speaking God. God wants to speak to everybody!

Whilst God will speak to you through the bible, this is only a general conversation which is applicable to all. It won't answer the very specific and unique questions about your life.

However, God wants to speak specifically into your life. To do this God gives the indwelling Holy Spirit to believers to work with your spirit to communicate with you. The Holy Spirit knows the mind and will of God and only speaks what He hears. The Holy Spirit will lead you into all truth.

God speaks to us in order for us to take the right direction and avoid pitfalls. Most importantly, God speaks to us for our good.

When you were born again, you entered into a relationship with the Holy Spirit who now indwells you.

Receiving direction from God is crucial to your success in life. The Lord wants to teach you and lead you in the direction that you should go (see Isa 48:17).

Do not underestimate the power of the ministry that God has given you. You are needed in the kingdom! You may never know the significance of the part you played in this life until everything is revealed in heaven.
