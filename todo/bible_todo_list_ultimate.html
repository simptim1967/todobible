The Greatest Commandments

In Mark's gospel (Mark 12:28) Jesus is asked "Which is the first commandment of all?" Jesus replies and names not only the first commandment of all but also the second, stating that there are no commandments greater than these. Jesus also says "On these two commandments hang all the law and the prophets" (Matthew 22:40).

The two commands in question are listed below and therefore represent the "Ultimate Bible ToDo List".

Note: This is the only Bible ToDo List that Christians are recommended to adhere to by this website. All other Bible ToDo Lists listed on this website are merely for encouragement and reminder purposes for those who love Jesus.
