The Greatest Commandments

In Mark's gospel chapter 12 Jesus is asked "Which is the first commandment of all?"

Jesus replies and names not only the first commandment of all but also the second, stating that there are no commandments greater than these. Jesus also says "On these two commandments hang all the law and the prophets" (Matthew 22:40).

The two commands in question are listed below and therefore represent what we call the "Bible Ultimate ToDo List."

Note: This is the only ToDo list that we recommend you adhere to. All other ToDo lists on our website are merely for encouragement and reminder purposes only to those who love Jesus.


Bible Ultimate ToDo Item Bible Reference
Thou shalt love the Lord thy God with all thy heart, soul, mind and strength. Mark 12:30
Thou shalt love thy neighbour as thyself. Mark 12:31
