About ToDo Bible

Dedication

This website is dedicated to the glory of the Lord Jesus Christ.

Welcome

Hello, my name is Tim Simpson from Telford, England. Thanks for visiting ToDoBible.org today.

This website documents my on-going journey, as a disciple of Jesus Christ, studying the Holy Bible to learn about what God has done for us, to understand how He wants us to live our lives and how He equips and empowers us to do so as Christians.

My quest is to grow in my relationship with Jesus and be taught by Him so that I can become more and more like Him. I want to understand the true meaning of God's Word, learn more about His character and understand the facts and promises as stated in the Holy Bible.

Every day I want to draw nearer to God so that He will draw ever nearer to me. I want to learn more about the miraculous power He has bestowed upon me and to see it working in the world as I live to fulfil His plan and purpose for my life. I want to grow spiritually and receive yet more revelation from His Holy Spirit that dwells within me.

This website is a work in progress and is continually being updated. I sincerely hope and pray that it blesses and encourages you. Please call back again soon.

King James Bible

The King James Bible (also known as The Authorised Version) is presented as a faithful translation of God's Word with the words of Christ highlighted in red.

My belief is that God's Holy Bible is not just something you read but also very much something you live, breathe and do, by God's grace, for His glory. It is to be read with the heart, not the head and is best done accompanied by the author, the Holy Spirit.

Not By Works

Nothing about this website seeks to promote salvation by works. Salvation is the free gift of God by faith in Jesus Christ and cannot be earned. The Bible ToDo List Items listed on this website are therefore not presented as a legalistic list of Christian do's and don'ts.

8  For by grace are ye saved through faith; and that not of yourselves: it is the gift of God:
9  Not of works, lest any man should boast.
10  For we are his workmanship, created in Christ Jesus unto good works, which God hath before ordained that we should walk in them.

Ephesians 2:8-10

The Bible ToDo List Items echo what the Holy scriptures teach us to do. Some also serve as sincere warnings or to highlight doctrinal truths and promises. All offer friendly encouragement acting as reminders to challenge the believer to incorporate them into daily living whenever opportunity arises.

Why not make your bible your ToDo list? Read it. Believe it. Do it! All for God's glory.
